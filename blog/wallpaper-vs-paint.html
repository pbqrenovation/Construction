Wallpaper vs Paint: Which Is Better for Your Toronto Home?

July 1, 2026 7 min read Wallpaper, Painting, Design Guide

One of the most common questions homeowners ask before starting a renovation is whether they should choose wallpaper or paint. Both options have unique advantages, and the right choice depends on your budget, design preferences, and how you plan to use the space.

Over the last decade, wallpaper has made a remarkable comeback. Modern materials, premium textures, and designer patterns have transformed wallpaper into one of the most popular interior design choices. At the same time, paint continues to be a timeless solution that offers flexibility, simplicity, and affordability.

If you're renovating your home in Toronto or the GTA, understanding the benefits of both options will help you make the right decision.

Appearance

The biggest difference between wallpaper and paint is visual impact.

Paint creates a clean and elegant finish that works with virtually any interior style. It allows furniture, artwork, and décor to become the focus of the room.

Wallpaper, however, creates an entirely different level of visual interest. Modern wallpaper offers textures, patterns, murals, and luxury finishes that simply cannot be achieved with paint.

Popular wallpaper styles include:

  • Botanical designs
  • Marble textures
  • Geometric patterns
  • Concrete and stone effects
  • Metallic finishes
  • Custom murals
  • Textured linen effects

If you want to create a luxury feature wall or make a room stand out, wallpaper is the clear winner.

Durability

High-quality interior paint generally lasts between five and ten years before requiring repainting.

Professionally installed wallpaper often lasts ten to fifteen years or even longer.

Modern wallpapers are designed to resist fading, peeling, and normal household wear, making them an excellent long-term investment.

For homeowners planning to stay in their property for many years, wallpaper often provides greater long-term value.

Cost Comparison

Paint usually has a lower initial cost.

Typical painting expenses include:

  • Surface preparation
  • Primer
  • Paint
  • Labor

Wallpaper installation typically costs more because it involves:

  • Premium materials
  • Detailed wall preparation
  • Pattern matching
  • Precision installation

Although wallpaper requires a larger initial investment, its longer lifespan often offsets the higher installation cost over time.

Maintenance

Painted walls are generally easier to touch up after accidental damage.

Wallpaper repairs can be more challenging if the original material is no longer available.

However, many modern vinyl wallpapers are washable and highly resistant to stains, making them ideal for busy households.

Both options are relatively easy to maintain when quality materials are used.

Installation Process

Painting is usually faster and less complex than wallpaper installation.

Wallpaper installation requires:

  • Accurate measurements
  • Perfect wall preparation
  • Pattern alignment
  • Precision trimming
  • Invisible seams

Even small installation mistakes can become very noticeable, which is why professional installation is strongly recommended.

Design Flexibility

Wallpaper offers nearly unlimited design possibilities.

Homeowners can choose from thousands of colors, patterns, and textures to create a unique interior.

Paint offers unlimited color choices but cannot replicate the texture, depth, or luxury appearance of modern wallpaper.

If your goal is to create a memorable interior, wallpaper provides far greater design flexibility.

Best Rooms for Paint

Paint works exceptionally well in:

  • Bedrooms
  • Hallways
  • Ceilings
  • Garages
  • Laundry rooms
  • Large open-concept spaces

Its clean appearance makes it suitable for almost every style of home.

Best Rooms for Wallpaper

Wallpaper is especially effective in:

  • Living rooms
  • Dining rooms
  • Home offices
  • Bedrooms
  • Entryways
  • Powder rooms
  • Commercial reception areas

Accent walls have become one of the most popular ways to introduce wallpaper without overwhelming a room.

Why Many Homeowners Choose Both

Today's interior design trends rarely involve choosing only paint or only wallpaper.

Instead, many homeowners combine both materials.

A common approach includes:

  • Three painted walls
  • One wallpaper feature wall

This creates a balanced design while controlling renovation costs.

Wallpaper feature walls remain one of the most requested services throughout Toronto and the GTA.

Wallpaper and Decorative Wall Panels

Another growing trend is combining wallpaper with decorative wall panels.

Popular combinations include:

  • Wood slat panels with textured wallpaper
  • Marble wallpaper with LED lighting
  • Modern moulding with wallpaper
  • Acoustic panels with neutral wallpaper

These combinations create luxury interiors often found in high-end custom homes.

Which Option Adds More Value?

Fresh paint increases a home's appeal by making rooms look clean, bright, and well maintained.

Professionally installed wallpaper creates memorable interiors and can increase perceived value by adding character and sophistication.

Feature walls are especially attractive to buyers looking for modern, move-in-ready homes.

Current Design Trends

Modern Toronto homes increasingly feature:

  • Neutral painted walls
  • Wallpaper feature walls
  • Wood slat accent walls
  • Botanical wallpaper
  • Marble-inspired designs
  • Minimalist Scandinavian interiors

These trends continue to dominate both residential and commercial renovation projects.

Professional Preparation Is Essential

Regardless of whether you choose paint or wallpaper, proper wall preparation is critical.

Professional preparation includes:

  • Drywall repairs
  • Filling imperfections
  • Sanding
  • Priming
  • Surface cleaning

A properly prepared wall ensures a smoother finish, better adhesion, and longer-lasting results.

Which Option Should You Choose?

Choose paint if you want:

  • Lower upfront costs
  • Easy maintenance
  • Fast installation
  • Flexible color changes

Choose wallpaper if you want:

  • A premium appearance
  • Luxury textures
  • Unique design features
  • Long-lasting durability
  • A striking feature wall

Many homeowners find that combining both materials offers the perfect balance between style and practicality.

Final Thoughts

There is no single answer when deciding between wallpaper and paint.

Both options offer excellent benefits depending on your renovation goals.

Paint provides a timeless, versatile finish that works well throughout the home, while wallpaper creates stunning focal points that add personality and sophistication.

If you're looking to completely transform your interior, combining professionally painted walls with a custom wallpaper feature wall is one of the most effective ways to achieve a modern, high-end appearance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is wallpaper more expensive than paint?

Yes. Wallpaper usually has a higher initial cost, but it often lasts significantly longer than paint.

Does wallpaper increase home value?

Professionally installed wallpaper can improve the overall appearance of a home and make feature walls more memorable to potential buyers.

Can wallpaper be installed over painted walls?

Yes. As long as the walls are smooth, clean, and properly primed, wallpaper can be installed over painted drywall.

Which option is better for resale?

Neutral paint appeals to the widest range of buyers, while tasteful wallpaper feature walls can help a property stand out in a competitive market.
